Israel Adesanya Wiki And Age

UFC fighter Israel Adesanya was born in Lagos, Nigeria, on 22 July 1989. He is 33 years old as of this writing.  His birth sign is Cancer, and he is of Nigerian ethnicity. 

Israel’s Father, Femi Adesanya, was an accountant, while his mother, Taiwo Adesanya, worked as a nurse. He grew up with his four other siblings in Lagos, Nigeria.

Adesanya’s family admitted him to Chrisland School, where he began his Taekwondo journey. Unfortunately, his mother forbade him from joining the club due to his injury.

Young Israel was bullied in school, further increasing his interest in martial arts as he wanted to defend himself.

Eventually, he attended Universal College of Learning to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Design. But he dropped out after two years and completely immersed himself in kickboxing.

The athlete traveled to China to pursue his dream of becoming a professional martial artist.
