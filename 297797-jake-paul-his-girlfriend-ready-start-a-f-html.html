Jake Paul has an on-again, off-again relationship with Julia Rose, a famous Instagram model. While Rose's boyfriend initially gained fame through social media, she had a different way of cementing her name publicly.

How Did Julia Rose Start Dating Jake Paul?

Rose and Paul were first linked in 2020 after they met on a music video production for "These Days" in 2019. What started as an on-screen love interest became a real-life romance for the YouTube star and Instagram model.

According to Paul, it was love at first sight when they met, and he spared no time confessing his feelings. "It was like six hours we spent together, and so I literally just told her, 'I love you,'" he said.

In March 2020, Paul told their love story to his social media followers, making his fans even more interested to know Rose. However, they had an unexpected breakup at the start of 2022.
