Professional Life and Career

Professionally, Patti Connors is a model and TV producer. She is also famous as the celebrity wife of Jimmy Connors. Her husband is the former world #1 tennis player who held the top Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) ranking for a then-record 160 consecutive weeks from 1974 to 1977.

Talking about her own professional life, Mrs. Connors is a well-recognized fashion model, production manager, entrepreneur, and actress. After completing her studies at Southern Illinois University, this personality started to work as a Bunny at the St. Louis Playboy Club. Likewise, she also starred in TV and print advertisements as well as commercial campaigns.

Moreover, she received the name of the “Playmate of the Month” in the November 1976 issue. This was for the popular Playboy magazine. Playboy photographer Pompeo Posar photographed this model for the November issue. The next year, the model received the “Playmate of the Year” Award in 1977. She received the award at the hands of the American singer Alice Cooper.

In the year 1978, the model starred on the popular back glass of the Playboy pinball machine. This personality was featured alongside the Playboy editor Hugh.M.Hefner. Reportedly, this pinball sold over 18,000 units around the world.

Furthermore, this personality also worked as an assistant production coordinator. As such, Mrs. Connors worked in movies including L. A. Confidential and The Bird Cage. She also worked on the TV show NYPD Blue. Additionally, this personality also assisted in movies including Tigerland, Batman Returns, V.I. Warshawski, and The Forbidden Dance. Most recently, the model released her range of food items online.
