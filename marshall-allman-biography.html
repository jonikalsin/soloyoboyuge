Who is Marshall Allman?

Marshall Allman is a well-recognized American actor, film editor, film producer, and screenwriter. Allman is widely famed for his role as L.J. Burrow on Fox’s successful television series, Prison Break.

Moreover, he is also renowned for his role as Tommy Mickens on True Blood.
