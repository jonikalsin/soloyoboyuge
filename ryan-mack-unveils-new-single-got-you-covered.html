Ryan Mack Unveils New Single 'Got You Covered'

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Talented singer and song-writer Ryan Mack, hailing from Kildare in Ireland, has unveiled his brilliant new single, titled ‘Got You Covered.’

Prior to the release of the new single, he took to his official Twitter account to share not only a preview of the track, but also a snippet of a music video for the single.

‘Got You Covered’ has an extremely unique and uptempo sound throughout, right from the first beat of the track. The build-up to the chorus is exceptional and also showcases his fantastic vocals. With such a catchy nature throughout the track, ‘Got You Covered’ will have you instantly singing along.

The new single represents a continually evolving sound for Ryan and he has done a great job with ‘Got You Covered’ – we absolutely love it!

Before embarking on a solo career in the music industry, Ryan was a member of Irish boy band sensation HomeTown. During their time together, the band achieved incredible success, particularly in their home country of Ireland.

Since the band’s separation several years ago, Ryan has been working incredibly hard behind the scenes on new music. Some of his recent releases include ‘Sofa’ and ‘I Don’t Wanna.’ His sound is described as being “a fresh new sound, which encapsulates pop melodies, soothing raw vocals and catchy, upbeat commercial music.”
