Has Alicia Navarro Been Found 2023? Missing Glendale Teenager Reunited With Her Family After 4 Years

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Has Alicia Navarro been found 2023? – Alicia Navarro went missing 5 days before her 15th birthday in 2019 at the age of 14 from Glendale, Arizona, outside of Phoenix. She has been found safe over three years later. 

Has Alicia Navarro been found 2023? Wiki, Biography, Age, Parents, Missing

Has Alicia Navarro been found 2023? – Alicia Navarro was born on 20 September 2004 in Arizona to her mother Jessica Nunez and her father Ivan Nunez. According to thedistin.com, Alicia has high-functioning autism.

On 15 September 2019, Alicia went missing from her home in Glendale, Arizona at the age of 14. She was 4 feet 5 inches tall and weighed 95 lbs when she disappeared. She was last seen in a white sweatshirt, denim overall skirt, and black and white Vans.

On a missing day, she didn’t contact her close friends and didn’t report to school and family. She left a letter to her parents letting them know she would return. According to KTAR, the letter read,

“I ran away. I will be back, I swear. I’m sorry.”

Alicia Navarro’s mother reported her disappearance to be out of character for her. She started to work with the Anti-Predator Project to get her daughter Alicia. She started to post on social media.

Alicia’s mother said on Fox 10 Phoenix,

“I am trying to do everything I can to keep her story alive. This is not a picture. It’s not just a picture, this is my daughter.”

After a lot of effort, Alicia’s mother never gave up and continued to search for her child.

Alicia Nunez said,

“So I’m living my worst nightmare right now. I’m just doing what a mother should do … not giving up and fighting for their child.”

Glendale Police Department announced on 26 July 2023 that Alicia Navarro had been found in Montana four years later.

A spokesperson said,

“She is by all accounts safe, she is by all accounts healthy, and she is by all accounts happy.”

“She went to a local police department in that area, she identified herself as Alicia Navarro, and at that point, our officers went into investigation mode.”

Officials interviewed her family before letting them know that the teenager standing before them was Navarro.

The spokesperson said,

“We are confident the person that we are talking with is indeed Alicia Navarro.”

Alicia’s mother Jessica Nunez broke down in tears as she reacted to the news on Wednesday while sharing a video on Facebook.

Jessica Nunez said,

“I do feel I owe this video to the community and to God. For everyone who has missing loved ones, I want you to use this case as an example that miracles do exist.”

Jessica said to other parents in her position to “never lose hope and always fight” confirming that her daughter “is safe.”

Jessica said,

“This is recent news for me. The important thing is that she is alive.”

Currently, Alicia Navarro is 18 years old as of 2023.
