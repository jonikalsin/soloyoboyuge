Meet Too Hot To Handle Brittan Byrd! Her Bio, Family, Job, Dating

May 2024 · 5 minute read

Netflix’s top dating reality show, Too Hot To Handles, is back with Season 4. Like previous seasons, it features stunning ladies and handsome men. Brittan Byrd is one of the cast members of Too Hot To Handle Season 4. Let’s learn about her dating life, age, job, family, and more in the wiki article below.

Brittan Byrd On Netflix’s Too Hot To Handle

In case you have forgotten what Netflix’s Too Hot To Handle is all about, don’t worry we have got you a brief overview of the show. As a part of the show, several young men and women are placed together in a resort where they have the ability to win huge amounts of cash. However, the contestants are not allowed to participate in any form of sexual activity, or the prize money will be deducted.

The task is harder than one can imagine, especially when there are beauties all over the resort. The fourth season of the show welcomed many attractive people. And they are Brittan Byrd, Creed McKinnon, Dominique Defoe, James Pendergrass, Jawahir Khalifa, Kayla Richart, Nick Kici, Nigel Jones, Sebastian Melrose, and Sophie Stonehouse.

Ahead of the show’s premiere (Dec 7), Brittan shared a small sneak peek of what was to come on her Instagram.

The reality TV star first announced herself as part of the Netflix show on November 22, 2022. Sharing a promotional picture of the show, Brittan wrote, “the countdown begins NOW ♡ catch me on #toohottohandle on December 7th ✨ @toohotnetflix @netflix @netflixuk,” on her Instagram.

Is Brittan Byrd Dating Anyone?

After Brittan made an entrance into the show, it was seemingly very hard for her to know exactly which show she was part of. Also, it was difficult for her to follow the different rules within the house.

However, somehow Brittan learned to embrace the concept of the show and thus began looking for someone to mingle with. She first laid her eyes on the sportsperson, James Pendergrass.

Soon, Brittan and James developed a connection. Though their connection had its fair share of ups and downs, they somehow kept the friendship intact.

At the time of this writing, we did not know if their connection eventually went on to take the form of a relationship. However, the duo did seem to be on good terms as they regularly commented on each other’s social media posts.

To talk a little about James, he is a “party-loving, funny, and irresistibly charming basketball player.” Also, James is said to be the youngest cast member on the show.

By profession, James is a physical therapist but he has done his fair share of modeling. The 6 feet 2 inches tall man also shared workout and lifestyle content on his popular IG account.

Brittan Byrd Age

As per netflixlife.com, Brittan Byrd’s age was 22 as of Dec 2022.

Brittan Byrd Family

Talking about Brittan’s family, her parents are Miemie Winn Byrd and John Edward Byrd.

Brittan’s mother, Miemie Byrd, 56, is a professor/teacher by profession. Miemie has been working for Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies for a long time now. There, she “researches, teaches, and publishes in the areas of U.S.-Myanmar (Burma) relations; security dynamics in Southeast Asia; economics and security linkages… women peace & security; and transformational learning & adult education.

Dr. Miemie previously served US Army for 28 years. In a long duration of 28 years, she became Deputy Economic Advisor, Civil-Military Operations Plans Officer, and Interagency Operations Officer at U.S. Pacific Command in support of Operations Enduring Freedom. Brittan’s mom received a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and Accounting from Claremont McKenna College. She completed her Master of Business Administration with an emphasis in Asia-Pacific Economics and Business from the University of Hawaii.

Too Hot To Handle star Brittan’s father, John Byrd, was 57 years old as of December 2022.

Furthermore, Brittan has one brother and his name is Newton.

Brittan Byrd Job
