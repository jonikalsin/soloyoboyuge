Japan McDonald's Pokémon toys

Two new sets of McDonald's Pokémon toys have been announced for Japan. The first set is available starting today, while the second set is available starting Dec. 20. The toys will be available in a special Happy Set box that is different from what is normally used for toy sets. When the Happy Set is bought on Dec. 14 or 15, they will receive a Pocket Monsters XY Pokedex Book ～ Xmas & new Year ～ and a a 36-page book full of Pokémon from the Kalos region.

The first set of toys will include:

  • Pokémon to begin your adventure 3-part stackable spinners
  • Xerneas and Yveltal Table Hockey
  • Eevee popped out!

The second set of toys will include:

  • Beat up Team Rocket together with Pikachu!
  • Rock-paper-scissors type battle
  • Pokémon Balance Blocks
